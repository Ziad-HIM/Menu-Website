About Ziad's Place

Founded in 1995 in the heart of the vibrant city of Miraville, Ziad's Place has been serving delicious and authentic Italian cuisine for nearly three decades. With locations in Miraville, Riverton, and Brooksville, our restaurant offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for family gatherings, romantic dinners, or casual outings with friends.

Our founder, Ziad (THATS ME!), envisioned a place where people could enjoy high-quality food made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Over the years, Ziad's Place has grown from a small eatery to a beloved dining destination, known for its exceptional pizza, pasta, salads, and desserts.
